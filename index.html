About

I'm a software engineer at Google working on Search Ads targeting. I also contribute to reinforcement learning efforts at DeepMind outside my core work.

Previously I worked on natural language processing (NLP) at Apple AI/ML for open domain question answering, and before that, I recieved a degree in applied mathematics from UC Berkeley. During my studies, I served as a nonvoting member of the Regents of the University of California.
